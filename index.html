The Ideas

A growing slate of projects. Each one solves a real, daily problem — and each one can stand alone or compose into a single citizen platform.

Climate · Water · Public Data

AI Dam Water-Level Monitoring

An AI camera reads live water height from a dam's gauge, plots it in real time, and watches the change day by day.

  • Fuses live level with heat, weather & rainfall data
  • Studies historical change and predicts future levels
  • Public dashboard: every dam's live level + full history
Police · Justice

Pre-FIR Validator AI

Complaints arrive as video, voice or text. The AI unifies them and identifies the correct legal sections before the FIR is filed.

  • Revalidates the sections an officer assigns from memory
  • Flags any missing section that should apply
  • Catches loopholes in the selected charges
Law · Citizen Access

Law AI — Public Legal Assistant

A helpful assistant on a phone number, WhatsApp and the web that lets anyone understand the law in plain language.

  • Answers any legal question, conversationally
  • India-wide, with Kerala-specific amendments built in
  • Always-on legal literacy for ordinary people
Enterprise · Tax

VAT Auditor AI for SMEs

Feed it a bank statement and a folder of bills — it maps every transaction and verifies the tax trail end to end.

  • Matches bills by date & amount, checks VAT is applied & filed
  • Cross-checks online ITR and validates tax per year
  • Advises on tax law, VAT category & refunds
Enterprise · Telephony

AI Phone Number for SMEs & Startups

Sign up with an Indian operator, get a number, and mount an AI agent that speaks for your business around the clock.

  • Explains the business and engages customers on call
  • Voice agent over an India-compatible SIP/telephony layer
  • No Twilio dependency — built for Indian telecom
Police · Maps · Access

Police Station Locator & Assistant

Every police station in Kerala on one map, with an AI guide to help citizens reach the right one, fast.

  • Navigate to the nearest or relevant station
  • Instant contact numbers & officer info
  • Jurisdiction, services & timings on demand
Welfare · Infrastructure · Accountability

AI Housing Inspection & Verification

When the government builds homes by the hundred, AI checks that every rupee becomes a real, livable house — verified against a standard checklist.

  • Photo/video evidence scored against a quality checklist
  • Validates water supply, sanitation & garbage management
  • Tracks construction progress & flags discrepancies per house
  • Stops fund leakage with an auditable, site-level trail
Utilities · Permits · Approvals

Permission & Permit Report Generation AI

The paperwork that stalls every project — drafted in minutes. AI assembles the approval reports utilities and citizens need, ready to submit.

  • Generates KSEB plan sanctions, water & gas pipeline permits
  • Checks each application against current regulatory norms
  • Flags missing documents before submission, not after
  • Turns weeks of back-and-forth into a clean first-pass file
Police · Citizen Safety

Citizen Crime Reporting AI

Reporting a crime should be as easy as telling someone. AI guides the citizen, builds a complete report, and sends it where it belongs.

  • Validates the incident and asks smart follow-up questions
  • Requests the right proof — photos, location, witnesses
  • Auto-routes to the correct station or authority
  • Pairs naturally with the Pre-FIR Validator & Station Locator
