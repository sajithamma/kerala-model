Environment · Water
DamSight AI
— Live AI dam water-level monitoring
An AI eye on every dam, reading the water in real time.

An AI camera reads live water height from a dam's gauge and plots it continuously, day by day. Fused with heat, rainfall and weather data, it studies how levels change, predicts what's coming, and publishes a live public dashboard of every dam's current level and full history — so flood risk and water security stop being a black box.

ImpactFlood preparedness, drought planning & public trust in water management — felt hardest in dam-shadow and downstream districts.

Justice · Police
PreFIR AI
— AI-based Pre-FIR validation
A second pair of eyes on every FIR, before it's filed.

Complaints arrive as video, voice or text. The AI unifies them and identifies the correct legal sections — then revalidates the sections an officer assigns from memory, flags any that are missing, and catches loopholes in the charges. A pre-FIR validator that makes the first official record fairer and more complete.

ImpactFewer weak or mis-framed FIRs, stronger cases, and fairer treatment of complainants across every police station.
Backed byKerala PoliceHome DeptLaw Dept

Health · Records
Quick Health Snapshot AI
— Instant citizen health record for emergencies
Your whole medical story, ready the moment it's needed.

A cloud health profile for every citizen, managed by AI. Records are added simply — upload a blood report, photograph a prescription, or auto-gather data from hospitals — and AI structures and maintains them over time, so an emergency app can instantly retrieve a person's latest medical condition.

ImpactFaster, safer emergency care and continuity of treatment — life-saving when seconds and history matter.

Health · Elderly
Senior Citizen SOS AI
— Voice SOS that gets elders the right help
An SOS that listens, understands, and gets help — for those living alone.

A phone app built for elderly citizens. On trigger, AI talks with the person in natural voice to assess the situation, understands the emergency need — medical, fall, safety — and connects them to the right responder. Paired with Quick Health Snapshot AI, the responder arrives already knowing the patient's history.

ImpactDignity and safety for Kerala's large elderly and "left-behind" population whose children live far away.

Safety · Women
Sentinel AI
— AI women-safety detection
An app that senses danger before you can even ask for help.

AI auto-detects a threat from the phone's camera and audio cues, assesses severity in real time, and pushes context — live location, audio/video evidence, a situation summary — to the nearest helping point. With silent triggers, auto-escalation if no one responds, and trusted-contact alerts. It joins Nearest Police Station AI, Citizen Crime Reporting AI and Senior Citizen SOS AI into one safety net.

ImpactFaster intervention in the critical seconds of an assault — a force-multiplier for the Pink Police network.

CarePoint AI
— AI govt health-facility finder
The public hospital that already has what you need — and costs a fraction.

Government hospitals often have facilities like CT scans far cheaper than private ones, but people don't know. This AI takes a person's problem or disease, identifies the facility or test they need, and shows the nearest best government option — with cost, availability and directions — surfacing public-healthcare value people are missing today.

ImpactSaves families from crippling private-care bills and fills underused public capacity.
